01 — The Tension

The booth deserves cinema.

i.

Millions spent. B-roll inside.

Maisons spend a fortune activating at Watches & Wonders, Only Watch, and the F1 paddock — then play product photography on loop inside the pavilion. The film inside the moment is the work that should matter most.

ii.

Generic agencies, generic films.

Stock motion templates, drone footage, watch-on-wrist clichés. Collectors can spot derivative work in a single frame — and the maison's voice gets lost in it.

iii.

Heritage needs direction.

Two centuries of horology condensed into a tagline disappears. Editorial direction is what restores the depth that justifies the price.
